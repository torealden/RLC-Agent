# RLC Comprehensive Commodity Coverage List

This document defines the complete set of commodities for which RLC will build trade data collection, balance sheets, and supply/demand models. Organized by category with the seed/oil/meal "triplet" pattern where applicable.

---

## 1. OILSEEDS (Seed + Oil + Meal Triplets)

Each oilseed complex has three components: the raw seed, the extracted vegetable oil, and the protein meal residue.

| # | Complex | Seed | Vegetable Oil | Protein Meal | Notes |
|---|---------|------|---------------|--------------|-------|
| 1 | **Soybean** | Soybeans | Soybean Oil (crude + refined) | Soybean Meal + Soybean Hulls | Largest US oilseed; meal split codes in 2023 |
| 2 | **Cottonseed** | Cottonseed | Cottonseed Oil (crude + refined) | Cottonseed Meal | Complete in collector as of Feb 2026 |
| 3 | **Canola/Rapeseed** | Canola/Rapeseed | Canola Oil (crude + refined) | Canola Meal | Low erucic acid vs other |
| 4 | **Sunflower** | Sunflower Seed | Sunflower Oil (crude + refined) | Sunflower Meal | |
| 5 | **Peanut/Groundnut** | Peanuts (in-shell + shelled) | Peanut Oil | Peanut Meal | |

## TOTAL COMMODITY COUNT: ~102 products across 9 categories

### Priority Tiers for Implementation
- **Tier 1 (Active):** Soybeans, corn, wheat, soybean oil, soybean meal, DDGS, cottonseed complex, ethanol, biodiesel, tallow, yellow grease, UCO
- **Tier 2 (Next):** Peanuts, flaxseed, canola, sunflower, safflower, palm oil, palm kernel oil, coconut oil, corn oil
- **Tier 3 (Soon):** All remaining oilseed complexes, livestock/meat, fertilizers, processing co-products
- **Tier 4 (Future):** Dairy, novel feedstocks, rendered by-products, energy petroleum
