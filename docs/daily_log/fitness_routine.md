# Fitness Routine (Mandate 3 — Fitness track)

**Locked 2026-06-08.** Weights/reps tracked in `fitness_tracking.xlsx`.

## Equipment available
- Half rack
- Dumbbells 3–50 lb
- Barbell + plates up to 45 lb total
- Rowing machine
- Exercise bike

## Baseline
- No injuries, no surgical history
- Aerobic base: ~1 hr Freddie walk daily (decent Z1/Z2)
- Strength training history: cold start — calibrate light on Week 1
- Tracking: actual weights/reps recorded after each session, adjust loads weekly

## Programming principles
- **4 strength sessions + 1 long endurance / week** (5 mandate-counting workouts).
  Off days still have Freddie walk; that's the daily baseline, not a Mandate 3 advance.
- **Active not passive bar:** session counts only if completed at target intensity.
  Skipping a planned set / dropping load mid-set is fine and gets logged; bailing on the
  session is not a hit.
- **Progressive overload:** add 2.5–5 lb to dumbbell lifts when prior session's top set
  hit prescribed reps with 2+ reps in reserve. Same for barbell.
- **Z2 endurance** on bike/row: should be conversational pace — could talk in full
  sentences. Most people go too hard on Z2.

## Day 1 — Full Body Foundation (today, 2026-06-08)

**Warm-up (10 min):**
- 5 min easy row OR bike (Z1 — just blood flow)
- 10 bodyweight squats, 5 push-ups (knees if needed), 10 leg swings/side, 10 arm circles each way

**Strength circuit (40 min) — 3 rounds, rest 60-90 sec between exercises, 2 min between rounds:**

| # | Exercise | Sets × Reps | Suggested start weight | Cue |
|---|----------|-------------|------------------------|-----|
| A1 | Goblet squat (DB held at chest) | 3 × 10 | 25-30 lb | Sit back, knees track over toes, full depth |
| A2 | DB bench press (floor or bench) | 3 × 10 | 20-25 lb each | Elbows ~45° from torso, full lockout |
| B1 | DB Romanian deadlift | 3 × 10 | 25-30 lb each | Hinge at hips, bar stays close, neutral back |
| B2 | DB bent-over row | 3 × 10 | 20-25 lb each | Pull to lower ribs, squeeze shoulder blades |
| C | Plank | 3 × 30-45 sec | bodyweight | Ribs down, glutes tight, no sagging |
| C | Glute bridge | 3 × 12 | bodyweight | Squeeze at top for 1 sec |

**Conditioning finisher (10 min):**
Row 500m → rest 60 sec → repeat 3× total. Pace each 500m around 2:15-2:30 (or whatever's hard-but-sustainable).

**Total: ~60 min.**

### Day 1 calibration note
Err on the lighter side for every exercise. Goal today is to assess what loads feel right,
not to lift heavy. Last rep of each set should feel like you had 2-3 reps in the tank.
We will jump loads next session if today felt easy.
