# MN MPCA Information Request — bundled facility list

**Purpose:** consolidate all Title V air permit + supporting document requests for Minnesota facilities into a single Information Request, rather than filing separately and burning the 21-day SLA clock per facility. Filed via https://www.pca.state.mn.us/about-mpca/information-request-form.

**Submitter:** Tore Alden — Round Lake Companies — tore.alden@roundlakescommodities.com

---

## What we're asking for (per facility)

For each facility listed below, please provide:

1. **Current active Title V (Major Air Operating Permit) document**, including all attachments and appendices.
2. **Permit modifications, amendments, and administrative revisions** from the past 5 years.
3. **Facility-wide emission unit inventory** (Form GI-05A and/or GI-05B equivalents), listing each emission unit with its identifier, function, fuel/throughput type, control device, and rated capacity.
4. **Any Compliance Assurance Monitoring (CAM) plans, Title V semi-annual deviation reports, and CD/CA compliance certifications** from the past 3 years.
5. **Construction permit documents** for any new emission unit installed at the facility in the past 5 years.

If a single document set covers multiple items above, that's fine — we only need one copy. Where the same permit applies to two facilities under common ownership at adjacent addresses, please indicate.
